Acer lucidum

Acer lucidum is an Asian species of maple. It has been found only in southern China (Fujian, Guangdong, Guangxi, Jiangxi, Sichuan).Flora of China, Acer lucidum F. P. Metcalf, 1932. 亮叶枫 liang ye feng Acer lucidum is a small tree up to 10 meters tall, with brown of grayish-brown bark. Leaves are non- compound, thick and leathery, up to 9 cm wide and 4 cm across, lance-shaped with no teeth or lobes. ==References== ==External links== *line drawing for Flora of China drawings 2 + 3 at top lucidum Category:Plants described in 1932 Category:Flora of China
