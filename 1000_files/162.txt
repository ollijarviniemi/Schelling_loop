Cyrtomoptera

Cyrtomoptera is a genus of soldier beetles in the family Cantharidae. There are at least two described species in Cyrtomoptera. ==Species== These two species belong to the genus Cyrtomoptera: * Cyrtomoptera dentata McKey-Fender, 1944 * Cyrtomoptera divisa (LeConte, 1851) ==References== ==Further reading== * * Category:Cantharidae Category:Articles created by Qbugbot
