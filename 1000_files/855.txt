Barbara Bolte

Barbara Bolte is a Canadian musician and music teacher. She is an accomplished oboist and pianist, who performs with many ensembles, including Opera York and the Kingston Symphony. Bolte played oboe and English horn with Penguin Cafe Orchestra. In her career, she has performed and studied on both sides of the Atlantic. She teaches at Queen's University in Kingston, Ontario, Canada as well as taking private students.
