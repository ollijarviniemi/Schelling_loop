The Informant (2013 film)

The Informant (, also known as Border Informant) is a 2013 French crime thriller film written and directed by Julien Leclercq.. ==Plot== Marc Duval is a bar owner in Gibraltar. He is cornered by his creditors, so he agrees to become a paid informant for the French customs to report drug trafficking. He quickly finds himself drawn into events more and more dangerous.
