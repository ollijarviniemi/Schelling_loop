Benessa

The Benessa (in Latin and Italian, known as Beneša or Benešić in Croatian) were a noble family of the Republic of Ragusa. Notable members include: *Damiano Benessa (Damianus Benessa or Damjan Beneša; 1477–1539), Humanist *Pietro Benessa (Petrus or Petar Benessa) *Andreas Benessa (or Andrija Beneša; 1295–1301), notary In the beginning of the 15th century Ragusan nobility were present in Novo Brdo in the Serbian Despotate as merchants or mining lords; Benessa were also present. == References == Category:Ragusan noble families
