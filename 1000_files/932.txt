Hesar-e Pain

Hesar-e Pain (, also Romanized as Ḩeşār-e Pā’īn, Ḩeşar Pā’īn, and Hisār Pāīn; also known as Ḩeşār-e Pā’īn va Qūrdlū) is a village in Tarrud Rural District, in the Central District of Damavand County, Tehran Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 421, in 109 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Damavand County
