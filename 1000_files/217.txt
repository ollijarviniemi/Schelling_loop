Igor Dolgatschew

Igor Dolgatschew bei der Premiere von "Son Mektup", im Cinedom Köln, am 19. März 2015 Igor Dolgatschew (born November 22, 1983, Novohrad-Volynskyi, Soviet Union) is a German actor. Dolgatschew began performing in theatre plays while still attending school in Magdeburg. He was soon cast in films and television shows like Wolffs Revier, Für alle Fälle Stefanie, Alphateam, Neues vom Bülowbogen, ' and '. In 2007, he won the groundbreaking role of Deniz Öztürk in Alles was zählt (All That Matters).
