Dagger Lake

Dagger Lake is located in North Cascades National Park, in the U. S. state of Washington. Dagger Lake is accessible via the Twisp Pass Trail and the lake is from the North Cascades Highway or from the Twisp River Road trailhead in Wenatchee National Forest. ==References== Category:Lakes of Washington (state) Category:North Cascades National Park Category:Lakes of Chelan County, Washington
