Niheli

Niheli is a Kurdish tribe and an area in Dohuk Governorate, Kurdistan Region in Iraq. Nihel is rather a small tribe and area comparing to other Kurdish tribes and areas, throughout history its people were more connected to Kurdish nationalism than tribalism.
