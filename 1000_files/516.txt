Shay Raviv

PR photo Shay Raviv (Yishay Rabinowicz) is a Los Angeles-based record producer, songwriter and music creative. ==History== Shay started playing classical piano at the age of six and discovered electronic music at age 14. He became passionate about electronic music and started experimenting with synthesizers and samplers. By the time he was 16, he had a studio of his own. At age 17 shay signed a recording contract with BNE records, and recorded his first three albums as "Violet Vision" with Jonathan Dagan aka J.Viewz.http://www.last.fm/music/Violet+Vision/+wiki With these albums, Shay became a well-known producer in the Israeli electronic music scene and abroad.http://www.last.fm/music/Violet+Vision/+wiki BNE Records were a major producer of electronic music. Their two main acts were Infected Mushroom and Violet Vision. Early Violet Vision songs were released in over 40 compilations worldwide. After pioneering the Israeli electronic music scene, Shay relocated to Los Angeles California,http://www.discogs.com/artist/27865-Violet-Vision where he expanded his musical interests. Shay's music has been featured extensively on various media channels including popular TV shows like House Of Lies (showtime) and Eastwick (ABC). Film music include The Chicago 8 and many more.https://www.imdb.com/name/nm3676282/ His remixes have always kept him at the forefront of dance music culture. Highlights include work for Infected Mushroom and Jonathan Davis. Recent musical material include songs with Miranda Lee-Richards,https://www.hmvdigital.com/artists/1908683 known for her work with The Brian Jonestown Massacre and The Jesus And Mary Chain, as well as new tracks with the Electronica group Terry Poison.http://blog.luxurias.net/post/62104733563/music-violet-vision-heaven- underground Shay's sound is deeply rooted in minimal electronics and often described as being emotional, personal and dark. ==Discography (Partial)== This discographyhttp://www.discogs.com/artist/520433-Shay-Raviv includes Shay Raviv's songwriting, composition, performance, production, music supervision, and remixes. ===Singles/EPS=== {| class="wikitable sortable" |- ! width="33"| Year ! width="130"| Artist ! width="300"| Album ! width="200"| Label |- | 2016 | Violet Vision | Diamonds (Feat.
