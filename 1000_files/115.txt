Porsuk River

Bridge over Porsuk River in Eskişehir The Porsuk River also Kocasu-Porsuk River (), ancient Tembris, is a Turkish river, that flows for . The city of Eskişehir is sited on the banks of this river. The river is dammed by the Porsuk I dam and Porsuk II dam forming large reservoirs.
