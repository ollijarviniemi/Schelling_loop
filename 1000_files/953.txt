Studleys Pond

Studleys Pond, known locally as Reed's Pond, is a pond in Rockland, Massachusetts. The pond is located south of Rockland Center on Route 123, west of Route 139. The French Stream flows through the pond. Gideon's Island, named after Gideon Studley, lies in the southern portion of the pond. Thomas V. Mahon Park, a park which is owned by the Town of Rockland and is accessible from Route 123, lies along the southern shore of the pond. ==External links== *Environmental Protection Agency *South Shore Coastal Watersheds - Lake Assessments Category:Ponds of Plymouth County, Massachusetts Category:Rockland, Massachusetts Category:Ponds of Massachusetts
