2012 Dothan Pro Tennis Classic

The 2012 Dothan Pro Tennis Classic was a professional tennis tournament played on clay courts. It was the twelfth edition of the tournament which was part of the 2012 ITF Women's Circuit, offering a total of $50,000 in prize money.
