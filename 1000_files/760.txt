1895 National Association Foot Ball League season

The National Association Foot Ball League, organized on January 8, 1895"Telegraphic News of Sport", Chicago Tribune, January 9, 1895, p11 played its first season in the spring of 1895, with four teams playing Sunday afternoon games. In the league opener on March 3, 1895, Centreville A.C. of Bayonne, New Jersey visited the Brooklyn Wanderers on the grounds of the Varuna Boat Club in South Brooklyn, and won, 5 to 2."The New League Opens Its Campaign", New York Sun, March 4, 1895, p.6 ==League standings== {| class="wikitable" |- !
