Ebrahimabad-e Jadid, Kerman

Ebrahimabad-e Jadid (, also Romanized as Ebrāhīmābād-e Jadīd; also known as Ebrāhīmābād) is a village in Chahdegal Rural District, Negin Kavir District, Fahraj County, Kerman Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 458, in 125 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Fahraj County
