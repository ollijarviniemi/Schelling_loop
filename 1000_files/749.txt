Round collar robe

Danryeong of Joseon Dynasty. Round collar robe (團領, pinyin: tuán lǐng, Hangul: 단령 Danryeong) was clothing of East Asian cultural sphere, which was used as Gwanbok. It was originated from China or hú (胡) and imported into Korea used as Gwanbok since Southern and Northern Dynasties of China. Category:History of Asian clothing
