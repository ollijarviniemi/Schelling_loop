Pirancheri

Pirancheri is a village in the Udayarpalayam taluk of Ariyalur district, Tamil Nadu, India. == Demographics == As per the 2001 census, Pirancheri had a total population of 1352 with 689 males and 663 females.Primary Census Abstract, Directorate of Census Operations-Tamil Nadu == References == Category:Villages in Ariyalur district
