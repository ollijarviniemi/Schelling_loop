Erika Clarke

Erika Clarke-Magaña (born 19 August 1981) is a former professional tennis player from Mexico. ==Biography== A right-handed player from Ciudad Juárez, Clarke represented the Mexico Fed Cup team in ten ties, between 2005 and 2008. She accumulated a win/loss record of 5–7. Clarke was a two-time medalist in doubles at the Central American and Caribbean Games and competed for Mexico at the 2007 Pan American Games, held in Rio de Janeiro.
