Naściszowa

Naściszowa is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Chełmiec, within Nowy Sącz County, Lesser Poland Voivodeship, in southern Poland. It lies approximately north-east of Chełmiec, north-east of Nowy Sącz, and south- east of the regional capital Kraków.
