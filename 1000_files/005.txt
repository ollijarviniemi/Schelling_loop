Campbell-Rumsey House

Campbell-Rumsey House is a historic home located at Bath in Steuben County, New York. It was built about 1855 and is a two-story, Italianate style brick residence. It was home to two prominent Bath residents, Robert Campbell (1808–1870) and David Rumsey (1810–1883).
