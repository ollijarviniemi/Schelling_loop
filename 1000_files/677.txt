Love in a Mist (play)

Love in a Mist is a 1941 comedy play by the British writer Kenneth Horne. The plot around a honeymooning couple at an isolated cottage on Exmoor. It premiered at the Connaught Theatre in Worthing. It then transferred to St Martin's Theatre in the West End, enjoying a run of 91 performances between 20 November 1941 and 7 February 1942. The cast included Ann Todd, Anna Konstam, Richard Bird, Michael Shepley and Marjorie Rhodes.Wearing p.55 ==References== ==Bibliography== * Wearing, J.P.
