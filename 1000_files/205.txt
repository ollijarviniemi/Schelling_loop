Sulejewo, Śrem County

Sulejewo is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Brodnica, within Śrem County, Greater Poland Voivodeship, in west-central Poland. It lies approximately north of Brodnica, north-west of Śrem, and south of the regional capital Poznań. The village has a population of 150. ==References== Category:Villages in Śrem County
