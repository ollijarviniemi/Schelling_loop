Gulnara Fattakhetdinova

Gulnara Fattakhetdinova (born 13 October 1982) is a former Russian tennis player. Fattakhetdinova has won 2 singles and 11 doubles titles on the ITF tour in her career. Her career-high ranking is world number 246, achieved on 12 July 2004 and her highest ranking in doubles is world number 102 on 6 October 2003. After retiring from the professional tour, she became a beach tennis player.beachtennisplayers.net Fattakhetdinova made her WTA main draw debut at the 2003 Kremlin Cup in the doubles event partnering Galina Fokina. == Career == === 2002 === In June 2002, Fattakhetdinova made her WTA main draw singles debut at the Tashkent Open, where she qualified for the main draw. Doubles in the main draw Fattakhetdinova partnered Ekaterina Kozhokina and the duo lost in the semifinals.
