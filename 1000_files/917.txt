Millerton, Kansas

Millerton is an unincorporated community in Sumner County, Kansas, United States. It is located about 5 miles northeast of Conway Springs at the intersection of N Maple Rd and W 123rd Ave N, next to the railroad. ==History== Millerton had a post office from 1875 until 1912, but the post office there was called Rolling Green until 1884. ==Education== The community is served by Clearwater USD 264 public school district. ==References== ==Further reading== ==External links== * Sumner County Map, KDOT Category:Unincorporated communities in Sumner County, Kansas Category:Unincorporated communities in Kansas
