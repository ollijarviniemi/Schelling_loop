Bruno Sant'Anna

Bruno Henrique Santos Sant'Anna (born 12 July 1993 in São José dos Campos) is a Brazilian tennis player. Sant'Anna has a career high ATP singles ranking of 337, achieved on 7 October 2013. He also has a career high ATP doubles ranking of 313 achieved on 23 May 2016. Sant'Anna made his ATP main draw debut at the 2012 Brasil Open, where he and Rogério Dutra da Silva received a wildcard into the doubles event.
