John Isenbarger

John Phillips Isenbarger (born December 5, 1947) is a former professional American football wide receiver in the National Football League. ==Football career== ===Professional football=== He played four seasons for the San Francisco 49ers, In 1974, he signed with the upstart World football league & the Hawaiians where he had his best season as a pro. Playing Wide Receiver he caught 33 passes for 368 yards & 7 touchdowns.
