Mangalya Bandhana

Mangalya Bandhana () is a 1993 Indian Kannada romantic drama film directed and produced by S. K. Bhagavan and written by P. Vasu.S. K. Bhagavan filmography The film stars Ananth Nag, Malashri and Moon Moon Sen in the lead roles along with Marthaanda, K. S. Ashwath and Ramesh Bhat in key supporting roles.Mangalya Bandhana (ಮಾಂಗಲ್ಯ ಬಂಧನ) 1993- Drama The film's music was composed by Hamsalekha.
