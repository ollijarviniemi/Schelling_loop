Nizhnesorokino

Nizhnesorokino () is a rural locality (a village) in Irsayevsky Selsoviet, Mishkinsky District, Bashkortostan, Russia. The population was 197 as of 2010. There are 4 streets. == Geography == It is located 6 km from Mishkino, 3 km from Irsayevo. == References == Category:Rural localities in Mishkinsky District
