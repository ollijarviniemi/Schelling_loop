Great Places

Great Places is a housing association in the United Kingdom, formerly the Manchester Methodist Housing Association. Great Places provides 19,000+ homes mostly in North West England.http://www.greatplaces.org.uk/SiteCollectionDocuments/Great%20Places%20Housing%20Group%20-%20map%20of%20where%20we%20work.pdf The organisation is an industrial and provident society headquartered in Manchester. In 2020, Equity Housing Group merged into Great Places.
