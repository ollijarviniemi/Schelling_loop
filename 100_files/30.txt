I'm Up (Young Thug mixtape)

I'm Up is a mixtape by American rapper Young Thug. It was released on February 5, 2016, by 300 Entertainment and Atlantic Records. The mixtape features guest appearances from Trouble, Ralo, Lil Durk, Lil Duke, Solo Lucci, Young Butta, Quavo, Offset, Dora and Dolly (Young Thug's sisters). == Commercial performance == I'm Up debuted at number 22 on the Billboard 200, with first week sales of 21,000 copies.
