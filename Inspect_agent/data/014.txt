Bas Paternotte

Bastiaan Paschier (Bas) Paternotte (born 1 September 1976 in Drachten) is a Dutch political reporter for the blogs ThePostOnline.nl (formerly DeJaap.nl) Profile on ThePostOnline.nl and GeenStijl and formerly for the magazine HP/De Tijd. and a political commentator on the PowNed radio programme Echte Jannen PowNed : Echte Jannen (segment: Haagse Geluid met Bas Paternotte) on Radio 1.
