Ghulam Ahmad Mir

Ghulam Ahmad Mir is an Indian politician from Indian National Congress who had represented Dooru (Vidhan Sabha constituency) in the Jammu and Kashmir State Assembly. He is the President of Jammu & Kashmir Pradesh Congress Committee.
