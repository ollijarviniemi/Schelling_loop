Leshi Singh

Leshi singh is a JDU member of the Legislative Assembly from the Dhamdaha seat in Bihar. She is a minister in nitish kumar cabinet and the president of Bihar state women commission. In the 2010 state assembly elections, Leshi Singh won the Dhamdaha assembly elections to become a Member of Legislative Assembly of BiharWinning Candidate Leshi Singh, Dhamdaha (in English).
