Exomella

Exomella is a genus of pill beetles in the family Byrrhidae. There are at least 2 described species in Exomella. ==Species== * Exomella merickeli Johnson, 1985 * Exomella pleuralis (Casey, 1908) ==References== * Johnson, Paul J. (1991).
