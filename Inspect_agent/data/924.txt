Datu Sikatuna

A monument of the blood compact in Tagbilaran City. Datu Sikatuna (or Catunao) as the Datu (chief of the Indianized Philippine polity) of the island of Bohol in the Philippines. His real name was "Katuna" but "Si" was added to his name as this is a nominative marker for a Filipino.
