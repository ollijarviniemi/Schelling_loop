Macropholidus ruthveni

Macropholidus ruthveni, known commonly as Ruthven's macropholidus, is a species of lizard in the family Gymnophthalmidae. The species is endemic to northwestern South America. ==Etymology== The specific name, ruthveni, is in honor of American herpetologist Alexander Grant Ruthven.Beolens, Bo; Watkins, Michael; Grayson, Michael (2011).
