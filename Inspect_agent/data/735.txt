Wierzbowo, Łomża County

Wierzbowo is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Śniadowo, within Łomża County, Podlaskie Voivodeship, in north-eastern Poland. It lies approximately north of Śniadowo, south-west of Łomża, and west of the regional capital Białystok. ==References== Category:Villages in Łomża County
