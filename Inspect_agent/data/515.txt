Keith Morgan (judoka)

Keith Morgan (born November 12, 1973 in Calgary, Alberta) is a retired judoka from Canada, who competed in four consecutive Summer Olympics, and won four medals at the Pan American Games. ==Sporting career== Morgan represented his native country at four consecutive Summer Olympics, starting in 1996 in Atlanta, Georgia.
