Digha (disambiguation)

Digha refers to a sea side resort in Purba Medinipur district, West Bengal, India It also refers to: *Digha, Patna, a neighbourhood in Patna, Bihar, India *Digha, North 24 Parganas, a census town in North 24 Parganas district, West Bengal, India *Digha, Nadia, a census town in Nadia district, West Bengal
