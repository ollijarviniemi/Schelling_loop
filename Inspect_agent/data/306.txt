Bongha Village

Roh Moo-hyun's birthplace in Bongha Bongha Maeul (봉하 마을; lit. "Bongha village") is a town in South Gyeongsang Province, South Korea. It is notable for being the hometown of former President of South Korea Roh Moo-hyun. It is located near Gimhae and Busan, in southeastern South Korea.
