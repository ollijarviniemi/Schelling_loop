Chrząstawa Wielka

Chrząstawa Wielka () is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Czernica, within Wrocław County, Lower Silesian Voivodeship, in south-western Poland. Prior to 1945 it was in Germany. It lies approximately north-east of Czernica and east of the regional capital Wrocław. ==References== Category:Villages in Wrocław County
