Mandhana Junction railway station

Mandhana Junction railway station is a small railway station in Kanpur district, Uttar Pradesh. Its code is MDA. It serves Kanpur city. The station consists of a single platform. The platform is not well sheltered. It lacks many facilities including water and sanitation.Line between Mandhana and Panki == Trains == * Kanpur - Farukhabad Passenger * Lucknow Junction - Kasganj Passenger * Allahabad - Farukhabad Passenger ==Development== The Indian Railway has planned to discard the railway line from Anwarganj to Mandhana and connect Mandhana Junction with Panki thus Mandhana will become an important railway station in North-Western Kanpur.
