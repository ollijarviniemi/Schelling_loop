Coșna

Coșna () is a commune located in Suceava County, Romania. It is composed of five villages: Coșna, Podu Coșnei, Românești, Teșna and Valea Bancului. These were part of Dorna Candrenilor Commune until 2003, when they were split off. ==References== Category:Communes in Suceava County Category:Localities in Southern Bukovina
