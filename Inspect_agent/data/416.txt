Chrasť nad Hornádom

Location of Spisska District in the Košice Region Chrasť nad Hornádom is a village and municipality in the Spišská Nová Ves District in the Košice Region of central-eastern Slovakia. ==Etymology== Chrasť - a dense bush, brushwood (Slovak from Proto-Slavic chvorstь). Horost (1280). ==History== In historical records the village was first mentioned in 1280. ==Geography== The village lies at an altitude of 420 metres and covers an area of 9.387 km².
