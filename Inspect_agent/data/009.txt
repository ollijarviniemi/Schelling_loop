Arba (Achaea)

Map of ancient Achaea (with place names in Greek) Arba () was a settlement in northern Achaea, Ancient Greece. Pausanias mentioned it as one of the villages that the inhabitants of Patrae fled to during the Achaean War.Pausanias, Description of Greece 7.18.6 Its location is unknown. ==References== Category:Populated places in ancient Achaea Category:Former populated places in Greece Category:Lost ancient cities and towns
