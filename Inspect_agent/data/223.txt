Alscheid

Alscheid (2012) Alscheid () is a village in the commune of Kiischpelt, in northern Luxembourg. , the village had a population of 47. Alscheid gave its name to the former commune of Kautenbach until 17 April 1914, when the commune was given the name Kautenbach, after its largest town.
