Mazraeh-ye Seyyed Murti

Mazraeh-ye Seyyed Murti (, also Romanized as Mazra‘eh-ye Seyyed Mūrtī) is a village in Dehshir Rural District, in the Central District of Taft County, Yazd Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its existence was noted, but its population was not reported. == References == Category:Populated places in Taft County
