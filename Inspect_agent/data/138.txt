Conant, Ohio

Conant is an unincorporated community in Allen County, in the U.S. state of Ohio. ==History== Conant had its start in 1884 when the Chicago and Atlantic Railroad was extended to that point. A post office called Conant was established in 1884, and remained in operation until 1919. ==References== Category:Unincorporated communities in Allen County, Ohio Category:1884 establishments in Ohio Category:Unincorporated communities in Ohio
