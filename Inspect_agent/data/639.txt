Bulloch County School District

The Bulloch County School District is a public school district in Bulloch County, Georgia, United States, based in Statesboro. It serves the communities of Brooklet, Hopeulikit, Portal, Register, and Statesboro. ==Schools== The Bulloch County School District has nine elementary schools, four middle schools, and three high schools. === Elementary schools === *Brooklet Elementary School *Julia P.
