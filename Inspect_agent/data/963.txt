Niedarzyno

Niedarzyno () is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Borzytuchom, within Bytów County, Pomeranian Voivodeship, in northern Poland. It lies approximately north-east of Borzytuchom, north-west of Bytów, and west of the regional capital Gdańsk. The village has a population of 253. ==References== Niedarzyno
