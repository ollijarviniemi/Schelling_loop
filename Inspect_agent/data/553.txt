Alina Soupian

Alina Soupian (born May 28, 2004) is an Israeli figure skater. On the junior level, she is the 2018 Golden Spin of Zagreb champion, the 2019 Open Ice Mall Cup champion, and the 2019 Israeli junior national champion.
