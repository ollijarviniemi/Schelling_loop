FC Arsenal-2 Tula

FC Arsenal-2 Tula () was a Russian football team from Tula, founded in 2012. Beginning in the 2014–15 season, it started playing in the Russian Professional Football League (third level). It was a farm club for FC Arsenal Tula. It was dissolved after the 2016–17 season.
