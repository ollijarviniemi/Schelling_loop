Solen lever kvar hos dig

"Solen lever kvar hos dig" () is a song recorded by Norwegian singer Kamferdrops. The song was released as a digital download on 26 February 2018 and peaked at number 60 on the Swedish Singles Chart. It took part in Melodifestivalen 2018, and placed sixth in the first semi-final on 3 February 2018.
