Lydia Molander

Lydia Molander Lydia Molander (; 1851–1929) was a Finnish-Swedish stage actress and opera singer. Lydia Molander was trained at the Royal Swedish Opera. She was engaged at the Swedish Theatre in Helsinki in 1874-1894.Svenskt porträttgalleri / XXI.
